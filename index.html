Why Yeida?

At NCR's Most Iconic Destination

YEIDA stands for Yamuna Expressway Industrial Development Authority and today has the largest industrial land bank in the country which would see rapid development. The initial budget set for YEIDA was Rs. 12,500 crore approximately. With an area of 2,689 sq km under its belt, it is the biggest industrial township project that covers connectivity, industrial development, and infrastructure development. YEIDA also promises to develop smart villages along the 165 km stretch. It is one of the driving forces behind Noida's growth and development. YEIDA's master plan of 2031 predicts rapid and extensive real estate development.
